💄 About Simran Makeover
Simran Makeover is a modern, elegant, and responsive salon appointment booking platform designed to simplify the appointment process for both customers and salon administrators. Built with React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Supabase, the application delivers a premium user experience with secure authentication, real-time database integration, and an intuitive admin dashboard.
Unlike traditional salon websites, Simran Makeover focuses on appointment management rather than online payments. Customers can quickly book appointments by selecting their preferred date and time, while payments are collected offline at the salon after the service is completed. This approach keeps the booking process simple, transparent, and convenient for both customers and the salon owner.
The platform includes a modern landing page, secure user authentication, appointment scheduling, booking status tracking, customer dashboard, and a powerful admin panel for managing appointments, customer records, gallery images, and business information. Designed with scalability in mind, the project can easily evolve into a complete salon management system by adding online payments, service catalogs, loyalty programs, AI beauty recommendations, and staff management in future releases.
